Discover Russia: Urals

Yekaterinburg- where Europe and Asia meet, known for its historical significance and beautiful nature. Visit places associated with the fate of the Romanovs, such as the Church on the Blood and the Chisty Pond.
Ufa- the capital of Bashkortostan, grab some echpochmak and kumys and set out to conquer the mountain rivers.
Chelyabinsk- the whole world knows about this place in the Urals! Do you know why the Chelyabinsk meteorite has not been seen? - Because it flew from the side of the sun.
